Overview of Xiamen City Medical

Xiamen is a coastal city located in south-east China, with a total population of about 1.5 million. It is a sub provincial city under the Fujian province. Xiamen City Medical is the host of the newly implemented MEDICS system. Which allows any insured citizen of Xiamen, China to be able to register for an E-Clearance card allowing the patient to make use of thier insurance at any Xiamen City Medical's associate facility. What became the Xiamen City Medical was founded by Frank Tsai of Xiaban. Providing the opportunity for those with insurance to use an e-clearance card at any of Xiamen City Medical's associate facilities. And is utilized by over 420,000 users on a day to day basis.
